## New Mods
- Silly Particles and animations of items in the inventory
- Visual health shown on players and entities

## Other Additions
- A new menu test (Available in the Silly SMP config settings)
- By default the "wifi symbol" is no longer visible on containers
- A new recepie for gilded Blackstone (1x Blackstone and 4x gold nuggets *shapeless*)
- Reduced download size from 789MB to 627MB

## Fixed Bugs
- Random crashes caused by Swinging Lanterns
- Crashes related to create mod contraptions crashing when assembled due to lighting optimisation mods
- Crashes and instability caused by a mod that allows you to see your crafting grid, offhand and armour slots in all inventory's
