## New Mods
- Discover plants, animals and monsters with a spyglass. Collect them all and take notes in your personal Field Guide!
- More mobs and variants
- Nether overhaul improving height based generation, adding nether ore spawning, new biomes and blocks, and new structures to explore
- Added the ability to attach chains to fences and use them as ziplines like in [Satisfactory](https://www.satisfactorygame.com)
- Sprinting in all directions
- Hotbar enhancements
- Recipe adjustments
- Right click harvest
- New particle effects
- lanterns now swing in the wind
- Added MCEF (Minecraft Chromium Embedded Framework)

## Other Additions
- New animated background on main menu
- Added "Silly SMP config" in settings:
    - Logo variations
    - Toggle to turn off animated background
    - Toggle extra loading screen info
- New loading menu
## Fixed Bugs
- Fixed the loading cube animation wouldn't always show.
