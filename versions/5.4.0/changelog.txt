## New Mods
- Your options are now persistant between updates. (***REQUIRES CONFIGURATION***)
  Depending on your operating system, options.txt is stored in a different folder.
  Move your existing options.txt from your 5.3.3 instance to the corisponding location for your OS:
  %APPDATA%\.minecraft *on Windows*
  $HOME/Library/Application Support/minecraft *on macOS*
  $HOME/.minecraft *on Linux*
- When mining blocks like Stone in the Overworld or Netherrack in the Nether, there is a very small chance that ores will drop
- A skills system - Open its UI with F4 key by default
- changed how sleeping works so now its animated and the ui is less cluttered
- New mail system - send items and letters to players
- New Underground structures
- Silly game console + mini games to play
- New death screen
- Auto connector to the server during restarts
- Crafting recipe for the Thigh Highs styles
- More mobs and mob variants
- Entity culling enhancements
- Smooth transitions between perspectives when pressing F5
- New Texture pack features that allow for customisation of sounds textures and models of entities.
