## New Mods
- Improved network performance and reduced bandwith usage with packet header indexing, aggregation + Zstd compression, delayed chunk caching, and persistent client-side chunk deduplication.
- Dynamic engine sounds for various create train types.
- Catenary wires, pantographs and more for electric modern trains in Create.
- Optimized texture animations. 
- Reduce fall damage by crouching just before landing
- Pixel-perfect, zero-lag hitboxes that instantly perfectly match the 3D visual models of every vanilla and modded entity in the game.
- Adds new engines to immersive aircraft, as well as other new upgrades

## Other Additions
- Restored The Omni mod to it's 1.0 to reveret these 2 bugs:
  - Fixed A bug where holding the sprint key while already sprinting would cause you to move faster
  - Fixed A bug where holding the sprint key while crouching would cause you to move faster
- Bushy Leaves Are no longer forced on and instead can be turned off in the resource packs screen.

## Fixed Bugs
- Removed the "Seamless sleep mod due to its incompatibility with other sleep related mods.
